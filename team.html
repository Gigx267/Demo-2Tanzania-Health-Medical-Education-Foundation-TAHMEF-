Tanzania Health & Medical Education Foundation (TAHMEF)
Founded in Tanzania since 2015, we serve marginalized Tanzanians identified through poverty, limited awareness, and negative cultural perceptions affecting their well-being. These are youths, those in the informal sector, rural poor, and indigenous communities.
Our Mission
We are a HealthTech organization promoting health equity in Tanzania through building and providing digital healthcare solutions.

At TAHMEF, our vision is to make Tanzania the epitome of health equity by ensuring that every individual has access to quality healthcare services, regardless of their geographical or socio-economic status.
